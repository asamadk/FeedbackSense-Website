Delivery and Activation Policy for FeedbackSense

Thank you for choosing FeedbackSense. We understand that access to the software you've purchased is essential. Therefore, this policy aims to clarify the delivery and activation process of our cloud software.

  1. Home
  2. Activation Policy

1. Software Delivery

  • Instant Access: Upon successful payment, you will receive an email with a link to access our cloud software platform. This email will typically arrive within a few minutes of your purchase.
  • Account Setup: If you're a new user, you'll be guided through a registration process to set up your account. If you're an existing user, simply log in to access the latest features.

2. Activation Process

  • Activation Link: In the email you receive post-purchase, there will be an activation link. Clicking on this link will either activate your subscription or provide further instructions.

3. Costs

There are no additional costs for software delivery or activation. The price you see during the purchase process is inclusive of all charges.

4. International Users

Our cloud software is available to users worldwide. The delivery and activation process remains the same regardless of your geographic location.

5. Assistance

If you encounter any issues during the delivery or activation process, please reach out to our support team at founder@feedbacksense.io. We aim to respond and resolve all queries within 24-48 hours.

6. Changes to This Policy

Feedback Software reserves the right to modify this Activation Policy at any time. Please review it occasionally. Your continued use of our website or software after the Activation Policy has been updated implies your acceptance of the revised terms.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding our Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact our support team. We are committed to addressing your inquiries and resolving any issues promptly.
