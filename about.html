The Genesis of Simplicity and Accessibility in Feedback Management

Welcome to FeedbackSense, a platform where innovation meets accessibility. I am Abdul Samad Kirmani, the founder of FeedbackSense, and our journey began with a simple yet powerful realization: the world of online surveys and feedback management is unnecessarily complicated and often out of reach for many, especially in countries like India.

Our mission

At FeedbackSense, we're committed to democratizing the survey creation and analysis process with our Free Online Survey Tool. We understand that conventional survey crafting methods, particularly the intricate conditional logic, can be complex and expensive. This realization propelled us to design a solution where you can Create, Share, and Analyze with Ease. Our Perfect Free Survey Builder is tailored for businesses, hotels, academia, restaurants, and more, ensuring that our AI-powered survey tools are user-friendly and budget-friendly. We ensure that advanced survey solutions are accessible to everyone, regardless of their budget, simplifying the survey process while maintaining professional standards.

Empowering the Budget-Conscious

Our focus is on small businesses, startups, individuals, and medium-sized businesses that operate with tight budgets but deserve the best tools to understand their customers. FeedbackSense is more than a survey platform; it's an ally for those who value their customer's voices but have been constrained by cost or complexity.

Innovation with Integrity

One of my core beliefs is that the practices in the realm of customer feedback and data management should be transparent and ethical. We've seen how data can be held hostage behind paywalls or how companies can be nickel-and-dimed by their service providers. FeedbackSense stands against these shady practices. We guarantee that your data remains accessible, and our pricing models are straightforward and fair.

Why FeedbackSense?

  • Ease of Use: Creating surveys with FeedbackSense is a breeze. The platform is designed to make conditional logic in surveys straightforward, allowing you to craft customized feedback tools with ease.
  • Cost-Effective: We believe that powerful tools shouldn't burn a hole in your pocket. FeedbackSense offers competitive pricing, making it an ideal choice for those on a limited budget.
  • AI-Powered Analysis: Harness the power of AI to analyze survey results, gaining insights that can drive your business forward.

Join Our Community:

We invite you to experience FeedbackSense. Whether you are a budding entrepreneur, a small business owner, or someone who values customer feedback, our platform is designed for you. Together, let's make survey creation and analysis easy, affordable, and ethical.

For more information or to start your journey with us, Contact Us or explore our services.
